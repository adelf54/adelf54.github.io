Best Baseball Stadiums

Top Pick

Image: San Francisco Giants by HarshLight at https://www.flickr.com/photos/harshlight/7911368670, Creative Commons

In my opinion, the best stadium to attend a MLB game at is the San Francisco Giants stadium. Oracle Park, formally called AT&T Park, brings scenic views, beautiful weather, and a great atmosphere all together in one place. It offers beautiful views of the San Francisco skyline and bay. Also, it has unique features such as the Coca-Cola bottle and giant glove in left field. There is nothing better than taking the train up to the Giants game and spending the day in San Francisco before a game underneath the lights
